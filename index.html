Frequently Asked Questions

What is Actuarial Consulting?

Actuarial consulting involves evaluating financial risks using mathematical models to forecast outcomes and help companies plan for future uncertainties. It is primarily used in insurance, pension, and investment industries.

How does predictive analytics work?

Predictive analytics uses historical data, statistical algorithms, and machine learning techniques to predict future outcomes. It is particularly useful in claims forecasting, customer behavior analysis, and fraud detection in the actuarial field.
